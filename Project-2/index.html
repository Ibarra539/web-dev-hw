You will need:

  1. One Tie
  2. One Wall Mirror
  3. Two Hands
  1. Start with the wide end of the tie on the right, and the small end on the left. Note: The tip of the small end should rest slightly about your belly-button, and you will only move the wide end.
  2. Move the wide end over the small end to the left.
  3. Move the wide end up into the neck loop from underneath.
  4. Then pull it down to the left.
  5. You will then loop it around the back of the small end to the right.
  6. Next pull up to the center towards the neck loop.
  7. Then tuck through the neck loop, and down to the right.
  8. Pull across the front to the left.
  9. Pull it up into the neck loop from underneath.
  10. Then back down through the loop you just created in the front.
  11. Finally, slide the knot up and tighten by pulling down on the wide end.

Once you follow these steps, you'll have a neat Windsor knot suitable for any occasion
